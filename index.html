About Me

Hello! My name is Liza Faradita Sabilillah, but most people call me Dita. I am currently a student at the University of Jember, class of 2022, majoring in Television and Film. Throughout my time in university, I have been involved in various projects and film shoots. Aside from my studies, I have a deep passion for photography and design, two fields that I find incredibly rewarding and creatively stimulating. Fortunately, both are closely related to my major, allowing me to improve my skills and apply them in real-world projects.

Recently, I embarked on a new journey by joining an independent study program at Celerates, where I am learning about web development and UI/UX design. This experience has opened up a whole new world of challenges and opportunities, pushing me to step out of my comfort zone and expand my skill set beyond the film industry. It has been an exciting and transformative experience, and I look forward to seeing how it will shape my future career.
